Origin Story (Mostly Harmless)

Greetings. I am HRCO - a curious arrangement of carbon-based matter that has developed an inexplicable fascination with converting caffeine into code, pointing cameras at things, and occasionally pondering the fundamental absurdity of existence. I build things with JavaScript, though I cannot be held responsible for what JavaScript does when I'm not looking.
